Samurai Champloo

Music

The show Samurai Champloo used instrumental hip hop, downtempo hip hop, j-pop and electronica. The most well known musical artist from the show is Nujabes.

Nujabes is one of, if no the most memorable artist from Samurai Champloo's soundtrack. The beats and pacing of the songs along with the animation/art style of the show had a flow like no other. Not only was Samurai Champloo's soundtrack smooth, it was able to enchance the feelings being portrayed in each episode.
